Seminar 5 – Dream Home Case Study (Dr. Godfried Williams)

Focus: Understanding database development through a practical property management example.

Key Points:

The “Dream Home” case study illustrates requirements for designing a real-world database system.

Emphasis on system methodologies, front-end and back-end integration, and evaluation of design solutions.

Stakeholders include property owners, agents, and clients—each with distinct data needs.

Discussion on consistency between tools, data models, and user requirements during system development.

Learning Outcomes:

Identify requirements for database-driven applications.

Apply design and development methodologies to real scenarios.

Evaluate system effectiveness and stakeholder alignment.

Build foundational understanding for subsequent SQL and DBMS topics.
