Seminar 1 – Deciphering Big Data (Dr. Godfried Williams)

Introductions & Module Outline: Overview of the course structure: introduction to data concepts, case study on data investigations, applications of data pipelines, database management systems, and deriving intelligence from data.

Assignments & Deadlines:

Team project (initial submission 8 Sept).

Individual project with executive summary (due 10 Oct).

Individual e-portfolio (due one week later).

All assignments are connected phases of the same context.

Big Data Rationale: Importance of understanding, investigating, and applying big data. The seminar sets the foundation for why "deciphering" is necessary—data is vast, complex, and requires structured methods.
